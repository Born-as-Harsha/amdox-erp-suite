# AMADOX ERP - User Manual

Welcome to the AMADOX Technologies Enterprise AI Powered Cloud ERP Suite. This guide helps you navigate and work with your personal user account.

---

## 🔑 Logging In
1. Load `https://amdox-erp-suite-gold.vercel.app/` in your browser.
2. In the **Email, Username, or Mobile Number** field, enter your email handle (e.g. `employee@amadox.com`) or your registered phone number.
3. Enter your password.
4. Pass the visual Captcha challenge by entering the exact case-sensitive characters shown in the image box.
5. Click **Request OTP / Sign In**.
   - If your role is standard Employee or Viewer, you will log in directly.
   - If your role is Manager, Accountant, or Admin, the screen will prompt you for OTP. Retrieve the OTP from the IT administrator console log, enter it, and click **Verify & Sign In**.

---

## ⚙️ Updating Settings & Profile Avatar
1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Under the **Personal Info** tab, update your address, biography,Emergency Contact, or languages.
3. Click the profile circle avatar card on the left side to choose and upload a new JPG/PNG photo.
4. Click **Save Configuration**.
   - Your name, designation, and new avatar picture will immediately reflect on the top Navbar header and the sidebar details card without any page reload.
